Filipino Fruit Seeds

Carabao Mango (Mangifera indica)

Delight in the tropical sweetness of Carabao Mangoes, known for their luscious texture and rich flavor. Cultivate your own mango tree at home and experience the joy of harvesting these golden fruits straight from your garden.

Davao Pomelo (Citrus maxima)

Experience the refreshing burst of citrus with Davao Pomelo seeds. These large, juicy fruits are prized for their sweet-tart flavor and are perfect for enjoying fresh or adding to salads and desserts. Grow your own pomelo tree and savor the taste of this tropical delight.

Philippine Banana

Embark on a journey to the tropics with Philippine Banana seeds. Grow your own banana tree and indulge in the creamy, sweet bananas that are a staple in Filipino cuisine. Whether eaten fresh, cooked, or used in smoothies, bananas are a versatile and nutritious treat.

Flower Seeds

Sampaguita (Jasminum sambac)

Immerse yourself in the enchanting fragrance of Sampaguita flowers. These delicate white blooms are revered for their sweet scent and are often used in religious ceremonies and floral garlands. Cultivate your own Sampaguita plants and fill your garden with their heavenly perfume.

Ylang-Ylang (Cananga odorata)

Discover the exotic allure of Ylang-Ylang flowers with their intoxicating aroma reminiscent of jasmine and neroli. These yellow blossoms are prized for their essential oil, which is used in perfumery and aromatherapy. Plant Ylang-Ylang seeds and create a sensory oasis in your garden.

Gumamela (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

Add a pop of color to your garden with Gumamela seeds. These vibrant Hibiscus flowers come in various shades of red, pink, orange, and yellow, and their trumpet-shaped blooms attract butterflies and hummingbirds. Grow your own Gumamela plants and enjoy a riot of color in your outdoor space.

Indoor Plants

Snake Plant (Sansevieria trifasciata)

Embrace the sleek elegance of Snake Plants, also known as Mother-in-Law's Tongue. These hardy plants feature tall, sword-shaped leaves with striking variegated patterns, making them a stylish addition to any indoor setting. Enjoy the air-purifying benefits of Snake Plants while adding a touch of modern sophistication to your home decor.

Golden Pothos

This trailing vine plant is renowned for its resilience and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it perfect for beginners and seasoned plant enthusiasts alike. Watch as its heart-shaped leaves cascade gracefully from hanging baskets or climb trellises, adding lush greenery to your indoor space.

Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum spp.)

Create a serene oasis in your home with Peace Lilies, prized for their elegant white blooms and glossy green foliage. These low-maintenance plants are known for their air-purifying qualities, making them ideal for bedrooms, offices, or any space where you want to promote a sense of calm and well-being. Enjoy the beauty and tranquility that Peace Lilies bring to your indoor environment.
