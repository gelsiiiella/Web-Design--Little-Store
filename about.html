Welcome to Halamanita, your one-stop destination for Filipino fruit seeds, flowers, and indoor plants. Inspired by the rich biodiversity and cultural heritage of the Philippines, Halamanita is dedicated to promoting the beauty and diversity of our native flora.

At Halamanita, we take pride in offering a handpicked selection of seeds from beloved Filipino fruits such as mango, lanzones, and avocado, allowing you to cultivate your own orchard and experience the joy of homegrown produce. Our collection also includes seeds of indigenous flowers like sampaguita, ylang-ylang, and gumamela, adding a touch of Filipino charm to your garden or indoor oasis.

In addition to our premium seeds, we specialize in providing a carefully curated assortment of indoor plants, chosen for their aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting your green journey, our team is here to offer expert advice and guidance every step of the way.
